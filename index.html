Cappuccino Coffee

Cappuccino is a beloved espresso-based coffee beverage that originated in Italy. Its name is derived from the Capuchin friars, whose brown robes resemble the color of the drink. Cappuccino is crafted by combining equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, resulting in a delightful balance of rich espresso flavor, creamy milkiness, and velvety foam.....
